I am the author of The Shulamite Bride. A prophet in the area of dreams and dream interpretation. A creative writer and Christian blogger.

I spent years of studying the word of God, building a relationship with our Creator. My heart is to impart everything that I have learnt with the hope that an
impact will be made to better our ways of living
through the word of God and it’s power.

God’s love is shared in all our lives. God’s heart is marriage, family, love. The reason for creation is to share intimacy with God and have fellowship in worship. I share content on various topics.
