12pm to 3pm:

Pray always in the Spirit.

Number 7 biblical number represents completion and a time to rest...
The heavens and the earth were completed 7 days. The number 7 is associated with perfection, wholeness, financial blessings, and rest.

Prayer Decrees for this watch: (Personal Decrees)

I have peace with God through Your Son, Jesus Christ. Therefore, I can enter into the rest He has provided for me. God has called me to testify of Jesus Christ to those whom the Holy Spirit leads me. God is above all gods. He alone is my refuge and place of safety. The Lord is my God, and I trust Him. I go to God for safety, and He protects me. He alone is my refuge and place of safety; He is my God, and I am trusting in Him. God rescues me from every trap and protects me from deadly diseases. He shields me with His wings! They shelter me. His faithful promises are my armor. Therefore, no evil will befall me, and no disaster will come to my home. God has put His angels to watch over me wherever I go. Therefore, I will not hit my foot on a rock.

I have no lack, there is no poverty in my family, my body suffers no sickness, nor is there drought in my finances. You supply all my needs according to Your glorious riches in Christ Jesus (Philippians 4:19). Give God the praise and honor for all He has done....
