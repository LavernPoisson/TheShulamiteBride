9pm to 12am:

Intercessory prayer, to pray for the protection of ourselves and those around us, pray for provisions in the different spheres of influence.

Romans 8:26-27 NKJV
Likewise, the Spirit also helps in our weaknesses. For we do not know what we should pray for as we ought, but the Spirit Himself makes intercession for us with groanings which cannot be uttered. [27] Now He who searches the hearts knows what the mind of the Spirit is, because He makes intercession for the saints according to the will of God.

I declare that no weapon formed against us will prosper and every accusation from the kingdom of darkness will be disintegrated in Jesus Name.

I lose my family, I lose ministry, I lose my loved ones and myself from every evil curse, spells, incantations, psychic powers and sorceries that have attached themselves to me or my family bloodline. We are set free, made whole, hidden under the shadow of the Almighty. I declare these curses are null and void, in Jesus’ Name. I come up against every evil alter where my name is mentioned, my family, my loved ones, my community, the schools, I make mention of the blood of Jesus, The blood of Jesus speaks for me and my loved ones.

I cancel every generational curse, cut off anything that is not of God that has come to frustrate the will of God over our homes in our communities, over our families. In the name of Jesus, I break off every power of the kingdom of darkness and cancel every proud idea that has established itself against Gods plans for my life. I speak against the Spirit of delay, I bring every thought into captivity to the obedience of Christ.

I take possession and speak to every sphere of influence in the name of Jesus, every sphere of influence will be occupied by Godly people that are in right standing with God. o Lord you said that we must occupy until You come.... ( Luke 19:13)

[20] And the Lord was very angry with Aaron and would have destroyed him; so, I prayed for Aaron also at the same time.
