MINISTRY

The meaning of the word "Ministry" implies a sense of being duty-bound to work under God's authority.

The mistake that many believers make is thinking that ministry is only associated to one part of their lives, and this is works of God pertaining to the Church, Ministry in a church building only. Ministry in serving in the Body of Christ using talents for worship, ushering, pastoring... when out of the Church building people go on with daily lives and forget to serve with the same tenacity or passion out of the church building.

A person that is born again, saved by the grace of God and comes to the full understanding of what a servant of God is knows and has a revelation of Ministry.

When one views life, we are all called to serve...Servants of God every action taken is ministering to someone or to a situation. So, when we are ministering to everything around us, Ministry becomes a daily consistent intentional dying to oneself and serving.

Ministry is not only in a church, but also daily living. The platforms that God has placed you in is Ministry... If you are called to the corporate industry that is your platform to minister. Through acts of kindness, through your heart of servant hood, through your acts of unselfishness.

If you are called to be a housewife, that is the place that God will use you most to share your gifts and abilities.

If you are called to the church to be a blessing to the Body of Christ, that is where God will use you to impact and impart.

Your core function will always be where God has called you, make sure to find out this place by spending time in Christ ...

Life will always be ministry to others, you have your core function but ultimately your life and acts of unselfishness will always be ministry to others. " That is Ministry"
