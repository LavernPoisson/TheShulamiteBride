Fourth Watch

3am to 6am:

Command your morning: Enter into the throne room, a time of intimacy in Christ.

Enter into the throne room become fully aware of the Lord being present with you... Be reminded of the Lord, He walked on the waters in the 4th watch, (Matthew 14:25-33)

Peter got out of the boat, walked on the waters keeping his eyes fixed on our Lord, forget about the storms and the sounds of the winds during this time stay focused on our King.... Spend time coming into complete intimacy, gaze into His eyes, wait on the Lord to hear if there is anything He needs to say.... gazing our eyes and keeping them fixed teaches us to trust, it was when Peter took His eyes off Jesus did, he begin to sink.

In this time spiritual sensitivity is extremely heightened, yes, the demonic forces are in full operation, the winds are extremely high, the evil is extremely thick... it is important for us to remember that just as demonic activities are high... the presence of the Lord is higher than any ruler or principalities of darkness... Becoming one with the father in this time allows intimacy to work on your behalf of you without any effort... Victory belongs to the Lord... You begin to walk on waters; you begin to rise above every circumstance you begin to hear the father direct you in the way you should go...

Those who wait upon the Lord in prayer, those who tarry in His presence, God will renew your strength Despite the winds that are all around Shift your focus and be still in the presence of the most high, Have you ever just waited on the Lord for a word knowing that word would carry you throughout the day... Wait in His presence and wait again...

Many want to warfare in this watch, but God is calling you to total intimacy in Him in this hour, He wants to renew your strength in Him in this hour, He will speak mysteries to you in this hour, Let His presence illuminates your entire surroundings as you yield to Him. He has already defeated death, hell and the grave.

Luke 12:37 NKJV
Blessed are those servants whom the master, when he comes, will find watching. Assuredly, I say to you that he will gird himself and have them sit down to eat and will come and serve them.

Supp with the Lord, be totally immersed in His presence... Allow Him to whisper in your ears, mysteries of deep calling unto deep... you have stepped out of warfare in the previous watch into His embrace ....

"The kingdom of heaven is like a man who sowed good seed in his field; but while men slept, his enemy came and sowed tares among the wheat and went his way," Matthew 13:24-25.

God is looking for Samuel's today who will respond to Him when the 3am wake-up call comes by saying,  he responds,

"Speak, says Samuel for your servant is listening"

Speak Lord, your servant is listening, Speak mysteries Lord, I am listening!!! Speak Lord, my heart awaits... speak Lord the answer is coming....

Luke 12:37 NKJV

This is not just an idea but a real provoking of the Holy Spirit upon God's people to ignite spiritual awakening, breakthrough and Kingdom advance in days of increasing darkness and conflict. Jesus is coming for an alert and ready bride.  Let us be ready with oil in our lamps, Matthew 25:5-7.

Let us fill our lamps up with oil, fill your oil lamps up for the road is long for there are traps along the way that only can only be taken out when our jars are full of oil...

Isaiah 10:27: “The yoke shall be destroyed because of the anointing.”

The anointing is found in intimacy with God; the anointing comes from having our lamps filled.
