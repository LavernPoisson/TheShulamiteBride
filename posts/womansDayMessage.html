A WOMANS DAY MESSAGE

  • A Message From God...
  • To the woman in despair, used by others for self-gain. Part of you feel incomplete, taken for granted, you have always given to others but who really gives to you? God sees you; For I have seen your efforts; I see how you pour into others how selfless you are. Never feel you are taken for granted, don’t you know that it is better to give than to receive, the moments that you feel discouraged or in despair turn to Me, I am your source, and I will pour rivers of living waters into your innermost parts which will take away the feeling of incompleteness, You are complete in me, you will give effortlessly I have gifts stored up that will never rust or disintegrate. You have been graced to give.
  • To the woman who is experiencing a sense of hopelessness, you have been struggling with a cheating partner, an abusive relationship, and a controlling narcissist. You have suffered rape and all sorts of mental and verbal abuse; you don’t know which way to turn. I know the struggles you have been faced with nothing is hidden from My sight, it has not been My intention for you to be inflicted or harmed, humanity has become wicked and lovers of self. I know the hurt and the pain you have endured whether you have chosen to stay in that abuse or not. No one deserves to be hurt."I see all things" Help is on the way, I have heard your cry, your tears have all come to Me, every last drop is stored up, you may feel hopeless..I am with you I will even make my bed in hell with you. The time is coming and is now that there is a recompense for every tear that has been shed. An outpouring of rain of many tears, but this time they will be tears of joy, tears of hope, tears of courage. Believe me. I set things apart, I also set things in order, and your affliction will not last. Help is on its way, won’t you turn your eyes on Me once more, I am the Author and Finisher of all things, I will perfect everything that which concerns you.
  • To the woman attending to her family, making the sacrifice of being the home executive. I am all-knowing, I have created you with a strong capability to fulfil a calling that is the highest form of servanthood. I have seen your sacrifice, I know your longing and desire for more, I am the Creator of all things, who would tend the field of family, who would impart into my precious seed. Who would reveal what the order of the church in marriage would look like if it weren’t for you, a woman of the Highest God? You are exactly where you need to be, you have been my brain in family and a true workmanship that should never feel ashamed. A warhead in prayer, full of selflessness, full of humility a heart of compassion. Don’t you know the demand and trust I have placed within you having to carry your family as you do, I know it’s not that simple, you sometimes wish it could be different for you, do you know that others also wish and desire to be exactly where you are. I have given you a task that could never be completed by any other. You are so special in my sight, your reward is heavenly, your reward is great.
  • To the working-class woman who works a 9 to 5 or whatever shift she must work to keep the money rolling in because she simply has no other choice. You are a rare choice of a fine diadem, I know what you have put up with in the work industry, I know your frustration and the times that you felt like giving up but instead, you pushed forward. You have left your kids at home, this has been painful as others grew them up. You have kept your head above waters, you managed to smile when deep down you cried and wondered, “Will I ever get that promotion, do they even recognise my capability.” You see I have graced you and strengthened you, I will continue to add grace and strength I need you in the field you are in who will reach out to the lost and broken if I had not strategically placed you in that position. Set your face as flint and allow me to promote you and open doors that no man will shut.
  • To the single woman who desires a relationship, she may or may not have a choice in her singleness. Many are called few are chosen, A married woman tends to her family tends to her husband. She has her place and yes, it is a beautiful place I have given her; but your place is just as beautiful; it is a gift that I have given both of you. I your God has called you in your singleness to partner with me we have so much work to do, you will be single minded seeing my plans and my purpose, see my vision you will be able to deliver my messages from a pure place being one with me, come a little higher come and see what I see then you will no longer wish for what I have not intended for you, Who can ascend to the heavens he who has clean hands and a pure heart, your singleness comes with purity and a deep focus for my heavenly design. It is a gift that I have created especially for you, can you please partner with me now that you have insight on what it is I have called you to. There are many more like you that are trapped, when they see your boldness and courage, they will then step out of the fear of feeling they are alone, they will see me work through you.
  • To the barren woman that so desires to bear a child but is without I am your God, who can fathom my ways or process my thoughts, Children are a gift from God a heritage and those who have them have, have their quiver full, My gifts come from within and come from afar, I looked the face of the earth to see the futility of this land but if everyone were fruitful then who would sow seeds of love, if I gave you your own would you stop caring for others. I made a way to bring balance knowing fully well that you would have the heart and compassion for others, who told you that you are barren, for I have not just given you one or two, but I have blessed with nations. I know that you will use this gift and navigate it the way I intended. In me there is no lack so you are not without. I bring good news of tidings and joy to the many sorrows that you once felt. Continue to give me your highest praise and rejoice for who knows the hour of my restoration, recompense or reconciliation.
  • To the woman that has studied day and night, to pursue her career. I am proud of you; I stand in awe when I see how you are using every cell in your body to grow and learn. Learning is a big part of my kingdom; you see in my kingdom there is constant room to grow and learn. You are exactly where you need to be. I want to remind you that I am the giver of all things and never feel that where you are in any way by your own achievement. It is I your God who builds kingdoms and who shuts them down, never feel that you are above any other ‘I will continue to pour myself In you as you continue to flourish, seeking greater opportunity in whichever field I will place you, I love that your gifts and talents are being sharpened to impact nations and empower many that I will set before you. I am pouring out My wisdom and I am gracing you for the task, so let your light shine before all mankind.
  • To the woman that has placed herself in business, who has succeeded as an entrepreneur. I have seen your perseverance, your stamina and courage. You have displayed a heroic role in achieving everything that I have placed inside of you. I am very pleased with your efforts and long sleepless nights of dedication have not gone unnoticed, you have stood in diverse situations overcoming many trails, there were many whispers of wanting to give up and yet you have stood the test of time. I have been with you throughout; I have provided you with every tool necessary to achieve the assignment set before you. I am the giver of all things good and bad, when I bless you with good things it is for my glory, it is for you to display my goodness through you, I have made you the hands and feet to help in places that only you can reach. Use what I have given you so that others will be drawn and learn that I am real through your acts of giving and nurturing what I have given to you so freely.
  • To the woman who has lost her life partner, husband, child. I know your pain, I know what you have endured when I gave my only Son, I felt every expression of loss and despair. After all you are made in my image, so I know what pain feels like. I cry with you every single time, your tears are mine, and your pain is mine. You may not have understood the loss you experienced or why you had to go through it. I want you to know I have created myself in you, when you grieve, I grieve with you and its okay to grieve, the reason I have given you tears to cry, your tears will heal you as time goes on and you begin to understand, it will take time, time also heals every pain; My heart is with you. I am close to the brokenhearted and to the contrite in spirit, I promise to complete you and be with you always. There is springtime there is winter, and in each season we are either sowing or reaping whatever season you are in; you are positioned to fulfil a purpose. Each one of you is destined with purpose and what is most important is to find the purpose that I have placed inside of you. I sent my only Son, the sacrifice of watching him carry a cross for the sake of humanity, for the sake of salvation. I cried with Him when He was nailed to the cross. That was His purpose, He sits on my right side reigning and ruling with me, When you find your purpose you will understand death, you will understand life, you will understand my plans, they are good they do give hope. Seek me and you will find life. It is the Glory of God to conceal a matter, it is the honour of kings to search it out. I have made each one of you precious in my sight a king in royalty. Search out my mystery of life with your entire being then you will be complete, then you will be fulfilled. Then you will know my ways. Then you will acknowledge that every good and perfect gift comes from the Father of Light.
  • Happy Woman's Day-The Shulamite
