FOR GOD SO LOVED THE WORLD

John 3:16

For God so loved the world that He gave His only begotten Son That whoever believes in Him will not perish but have eternal life.

God gave because He loved. Love gives...
Perhaps you can identify with what I'm saying; when you are in Christ, His love causes you to give. It's because love gives...
Love propels....
Love propels people to do things that they wouldn't necessarily do... it is the love of God. God has given His Love and shown us His Love; it is His Love that gives us tolerance for situations that in the natural our minds would not be able to deal with.

It is a supernatural force of love that enables us. Love enables, God's enabling love quickens our spirit to feel, be conscious, and be self-aware. These aspects of you cannot be played down or acted out. Love is felt; by no means can anyone try to act or pretend love because your spirit will know. That is the beauty of this love that God has given; when you experience His love, you will never be fooled by anyone that falsely pretends to love you. Your experience of His love teaches you this.

I go out and do street evangelism each Saturday. it is not because I dont have a life and have nothing else to do; it is the Love of God that is shared abroad in my heart through Jesus, through acceptance in Christ that causes me, along with a group of other believers, to give of our time. We were all lost living in our sinful nature until we experienced the love of God. Through the Love of Christ, He propels and enables us to share with others so that they too can share in and experience the Love of God.

We are all partakers of the Kingdom sharing His Love; there were others that took time to minister and pray for me. As believers in Christ, we are commissioned to win souls by showing love...some people in this world will only ever see Jesus through us.

Here are a few examples found in scripture, showing us that " Love Gives."

John 10:10 NKJV
The thief does not come except to steal, and to kill, and to destroy. I have come that they may have life, and that they may have it more abundantly.

God has GIVEN us life... and the scripture says life more abundantly.

Psalm 29:11: The Lord GIVES strength to his people; the Lord blesses his people with peace.

Genesis 1:26
God said, "Let them have dominion..." "Authority and responsibility to govern and steward your surroundings—these include your body. Mind and soul... He who has dominion over his mind has dominion over his surroundings.

God has GIVEN us dominion to rule over our environment. Love gave wisdom to King Solomon (1st Kings 3).

Proverbs 2:6: For the Lord GIVES wisdom; from his mouth come knowledge and understanding.

James 1:5
If any of you lacks wisdom, you should ask God, who GIVES generously to all without finding fault, and it will be given to you.

So we see that love GIVES...
God GAVE His only son so that we could be set free. When He gave Jesus to us, He bore our sin, our sickness, all on the cross of Calvary... There was an exchange that took place, an exchange of God's love; that love was exchanged again on the cross... He took on our sins and yet knew no sin.

Love is always exchanging; love is always sacrificing, and yet it never loses its power/essence... True love is the God.
